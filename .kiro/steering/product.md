# Product Overview

DentalPro Manager is a comprehensive dental practice management system designed to streamline clinic operations and increase efficiency.

## Core Features

- **Patient Management**: Complete patient records, medical history, and treatment planning
- **Appointment Scheduling**: Advanced scheduling with conflict detection, automated reminders (SMS/Email), and real-time availability
- **Billing & Financial**: Automated invoicing, insurance claims processing, payment tracking, and revenue analytics
- **Medications & Prescriptions**: Digital prescription management with medication catalog
- **Multi-Clinic Support**: Centralized management for multiple locations with unified patient records
- **Staff Scheduling**: Cross-clinic staff scheduling and availability management
- **Reports & Analytics**: Financial insights, appointment analytics, and performance metrics

## Target Users

- Solo dental practitioners
- Group dental practices
- Multi-location dental clinics
- Dental service organizations (DSOs)

## Key Business Goals

- Improve scheduling efficiency and reduce no-shows
- Automate administrative tasks
- Enhance patient satisfaction through streamlined booking
- Provide comprehensive financial tracking and reporting
- Support HIPAA-compliant data handling
