## Introduction

Welcome to DentalPro Manager, your comprehensive dental practice management solution. This guide will help you navigate the system and make the most of its features to streamline your dental practice operations.

## Getting Started

### System Requirements

- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- Internet connection
- Screen resolution of at least 1280x720

### Accessing the System

1. Open your web browser and navigate to the DentalPro Manager URL provided by your administrator
2. You will be directed to the login page

### Creating an Account

1. Click on the "Register" button on the login page
2. Fill in your details (name, email, password)
3. Select your role (if applicable)
4. Click "Create Account"
5. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your email

### Logging In

1. Enter your email address and password
2. Click "Login"
3. If you've forgotten your password, click "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions

## Dashboard

The dashboard is your central hub for daily operations, providing an overview of your practice's key metrics and activities.

### Dashboard Components

- **Appointment Overview**: Today's appointments and upcoming schedule
- **Patient Statistics**: New patients, total patients, and patient demographics
- **Financial Summary**: Recent transactions, outstanding balances, and revenue trends
- **Task List**: Pending tasks and reminders
- **Recent Activity**: Latest actions in the system

### Customizing Your Dashboard

1. Click the "Customize" button in the top-right corner
2. Drag and drop widgets to rearrange them
3. Click the gear icon on any widget to configure its settings
4. Click "Save Layout" when finished

## Patient Management

### Adding a New Patient

1. Navigate to the "Patients" section from the main menu
2. Click the "Add New Patient" button
3. Fill in the patient's personal information (name, contact details, date of birth)
4. Add medical history, insurance information, and other relevant details
5. Click "Save" to create the patient record

### Searching for Patients

1. Use the search bar at the top of the Patients page
2. Enter the patient's name, phone number, or email
3. Click on a patient from the search results to view their profile

### Viewing Patient Details

The patient profile includes:

- **Personal Information**: Contact details, demographics, and emergency contacts
- **Medical History**: Allergies, conditions, and medications
- **Dental History**: Previous treatments and procedures
- **Appointment History**: Past and upcoming appointments
- **Billing Information**: Insurance details and payment history
- **Treatment Plans**: Current and past treatment plans

### Updating Patient Information

1. Navigate to the patient's profile
2. Click the "Edit" button in the section you want to update
3. Make the necessary changes
4. Click "Save" to update the record

## Appointment Scheduling

### Viewing the Appointment Calendar

1. Navigate to the "Appointments" section from the main menu
2. The default view shows today's appointments
3. Use the calendar navigation to switch between day, week, and month views
4. Click on any date to view appointments for that day

### Creating a New Appointment

1. Click the "New Appointment" button or click on an empty time slot in the calendar
2. Select a patient from the dropdown or search for a patient
3. Choose the appointment type, date, and time
4. Add any notes or special instructions
5. Click "Save" to create the appointment

### Managing Appointments

- **Rescheduling**: Click on an appointment and select "Reschedule"
- **Cancelling**: Click on an appointment and select "Cancel"
- **Marking as Completed**: Click on an appointment and select "Mark as Completed"
- **Adding Notes**: Click on an appointment and select "Add Notes"

### Setting Up Recurring Appointments

1. Create a new appointment as usual
2. Check the "Recurring" option
3. Set the recurrence pattern (weekly, monthly, etc.)
4. Specify the end date or number of occurrences
5. Click "Save"

## Billing and Payments

### Creating an Invoice

1. Navigate to the "Billing" section from the main menu
2. Click "New Invoice"
3. Select a patient from the dropdown
4. Add services or items to the invoice
5. Apply any discounts or adjustments
6. Click "Save" to create the invoice
7. Optionally, click "Send to Patient" to email the invoice

### Processing Payments

1. Navigate to the "Billing" section
2. Find the invoice you want to process payment for
3. Click "Record Payment"
4. Enter the payment amount, method, and date
5. Add any notes if necessary
6. Click "Save" to record the payment

### Managing Insurance Claims

1. Navigate to the "Billing" section
2. Click on the "Insurance Claims" tab
3. Click "New Claim" to create a new insurance claim
4. Select the patient and related invoices
5. Enter the insurance details
6. Click "Submit Claim"

### Viewing Financial Reports

1. Navigate to the "Reports" section
2. Select "Financial Reports" from the categories
3. Choose the report type (revenue, outstanding balances, etc.)
4. Set the date range and other filters
5. Click "Generate Report"

## Medication Management

### Adding Medications to the Catalog

1. Navigate to the "Medications" section
2. Click "Add Medication"
3. Enter the medication details (name, dosage, form, etc.)
4. Add information about contraindications and side effects
5. Click "Save"

### Searching for Medications

1. Use the search bar in the Medications section
2. Enter the medication name or active ingredient
3. Filter results by category if needed

## Prescription Management

### Creating a New Prescription

1. Navigate to the "Prescriptions" section or the patient's profile
2. Click "New Prescription"
3. Select a patient if not already selected
4. Add medications from the catalog
5. Specify dosage, frequency, and duration for each medication
6. Add special instructions if needed
7. Click "Save" to create the prescription
8. Optionally, click "Print" to print the prescription

### Viewing Prescription History

1. Navigate to the patient's profile
2. Click on the "Prescriptions" tab
3. View all past and current prescriptions

## Reports

### Available Report Types

- **Patient Reports**: Demographics, new patients, patient activity
- **Appointment Reports**: Scheduling statistics, no-shows, cancellations
- **Financial Reports**: Revenue, outstanding balances, insurance claims
- **Treatment Reports**: Procedures performed, treatment outcomes
- **Staff Reports**: Provider productivity, scheduling efficiency

### Generating Reports

1. Navigate to the "Reports" section
2. Select the report category and specific report
3. Set the date range and other filters
4. Click "Generate Report"
5. View the report on screen or export it

### Exporting Reports

1. Generate the report as described above
2. Click the "Export" button
3. Choose the format (PDF, Excel, CSV)
4. Save the file to your computer

## Multi-Clinic Management

### Switching Between Clinics

1. Click on the clinic selector in the top navigation bar
2. Choose the clinic you want to view from the dropdown
3. The interface will update to show data for the selected clinic

### Managing Clinic Settings

1. Navigate to the "Settings" section
2. Select "Clinic Settings"
3. Choose the clinic you want to manage
4. Update clinic details, operating hours, and other settings
5. Click "Save"

### Clinic-Specific Reports

1. Navigate to the "Reports" section
2. Select the report type
3. Use the clinic filter to select a specific clinic or view all clinics
4. Generate the report as usual

## Settings

### User Profile Settings

1. Click on your profile picture or name in the top-right corner
2. Select "Profile Settings"
3. Update your personal information, contact details, and preferences
4. Click "Save"

### Notification Settings

1. Navigate to the "Settings" section
2. Select "Notification Settings"
3. Configure which notifications you want to receive and how (email, in-app)
4. Click "Save"

### System Settings

*Note: System settings are only available to administrators*

1. Navigate to the "Settings" section
2. Select "System Settings"
3. Configure global settings for the practice
4. Click "Save"

### User Management

*Note: User management is only available to administrators*

1. Navigate to the "Settings" section
2. Select "User Management"
3. View, add, edit, or deactivate user accounts
4. Manage user roles and permissions

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Login Problems

- **Forgotten Password**: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page
- **Account Locked**: Contact your system administrator
- **Browser Issues**: Clear your browser cache or try a different browser

#### System Performance

- Ensure you have a stable internet connection
- Close unnecessary browser tabs and applications
- Clear your browser cache regularly

### Getting Help

- Click the "Help" icon in the top navigation bar
- Browse the knowledge base for tutorials and FAQs
- Contact support through the in-app chat or email
